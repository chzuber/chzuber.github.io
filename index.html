Christopher Zuber

Head of Unit at SVR Wirtschaft

I am head of the macroeconomics unit at the German Council of Economic Experts (GCEE).

My research focuses on the effect of fiscal policy and the link between recessions and potential output revisions. In addition, I work on policy issues related to fiscal policy such as fiscal rules, the German debt brake, and fiscal space.
